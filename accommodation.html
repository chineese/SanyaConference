Visa Issues


Hainan Island Visa Application

  1. Passengers who enjoy visa-free access in Phoenix airport:
    • Since commencement of operations, Sanya Phoenix International Airport Entry and Exit has provided a landing visa policy. Passengers from 26 countries can enjoy this “visa-free” policy: Japan, Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, South Korea, Philippines, Indonesia, Germany, England, France, Austria, Italy, Russia, Switzerland, Sweden, Spain, Holland, the United States, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Ukraine, Kazakhstan, Finland, Norway, and Denmark. Groups of more than two passengers from Russia, Japan and Korea can extend their stay from 15 to 21 days. These policies make it convenient for passenger traveling to Sanya.

  2. Sanya Phoenix International Airport was approved by the State Council in 1995, and is the southernmost level-one airport in China. Facilities include customs, frontier inspection, quarantine, public security visa and other departments. Visa matters are processed by the Public Security Bureau, and there is a preferential visa policy for passengers arriving on international business and tourist flights. The valid period of a landing visa is one month and passengers holding this visa can leave the country from any domestic airport.
